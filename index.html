Hair Day - Your Fun, Fresh Salon

Located in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a small, cozy salon owned by colorist Pria and hairdresser Garry. We’re known for cool hair colors, trendy cuts, and a chill vibe. With limited coloring spots each week, book early and check out our makeup and nail tutorials online!

About Us

Hair Day was born from Pria’s passion for colorful, creative hair and Garry’s talent for cutting and styling. After earning her colorist certificate, Pria dreamed of a salon where clients could “treat themselves” to something special.

She teamed up with Garry, her longtime friend, to open Hair Day in Madison. Our tight-knit crew of seven—two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist, and one make-up artist—works together to bring you standout looks in a fun, relaxed setting. We love sharing our skills, whether it’s through a bold dye job or online tutorials to keep you glowing between appointments!

What We Offer?

Cuts, Colors & Styling

From sharp, trendy cuts to vibrant, one-of-a-kind colors, our stylists and colorists have you covered. Whether you want a total transformation or a quick refresh, we’ll make it happen with care and flair.

Makeup & Nails

Complete your look with our makeup and nail services! Our make-up artist can create anything from natural glows to bold statements, while our manicurist keeps your nails on point—perfect for a special day or just because.
